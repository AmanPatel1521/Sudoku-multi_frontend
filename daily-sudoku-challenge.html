🧠 How to Approach the Daily Challenge

Before you start, keep these tips in mind:

  • Begin with rows or columns that have the most numbers filled
  • Use elimination to identify missing values
  • Focus on 3×3 boxes for quick placements
  • Avoid guessing—always rely on logic
