Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Multiplayer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains that our application does absolutely no aggressive data harvesting, selling, or tracking.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal identities, emails, or device identifiers.
The only data temporarily processed by our application is your chosen display "Player Name" and gameplay statistics (Match Duration and Accuracy) which are required for the global multiplayer leaderboard to function.

3. Local Storage

Your display name, avatar, and offline Solo statistics are stored securely on your local device using standard browser LocalStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you actively participate in a multiplayer match.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party advertising SDKs or analytics trackers (such as Google Analytics or AdMob). We do not share any gameplay data with external advertising networks.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. By continuing to use the app, you agree to any fundamental changes in standard player functionality.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer directly.
