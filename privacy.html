1. Introduction

Welcome to Multiplayer Sudoku, developed and operated by Aman Patel. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal identities, emails, or device identifiers.

The only data processed by our application is your chosen display "Player Name" and gameplay statistics (Match Duration, Score, and Accuracy). This data is required solely for the global multiplayer leaderboard to function properly.

3. Local Storage & Cookies

Your display name, chosen avatar, and offline Solo statistics are stored securely on your local device using standard browser LocalStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you actively participate in a multiplayer match.

We and our third-party partners (such as Google AdSense) use cookies to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements to our users. To comply with Google's policies, please be aware of the following:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting youradchoices.com.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Any generic aggregated demographic information is only used by third-party advertising platforms (like Google AdSense) to serve relevant display ads.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes. Your continued use of the site following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your dealings with this site, please contact us via the Contact page.
