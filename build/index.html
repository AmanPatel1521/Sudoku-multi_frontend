        How to Play Sudoku Online

        Sudoku is based on a simple rule:

        • Fill a 9×9 grid using numbers from 1 to 9
        • Each row must contain all numbers without repetition
        • Each column must contain all numbers without repetition
        • Each 3×3 box must include numbers 1 to 9 exactly once

        In multiplayer mode:

        • You play against other users solving the same puzzle
        • Faster and more accurate players rank higher
        • Mistakes can affect your score and ranking

        Time Limits: Games are timed based on difficulty (Easy: 8 min, Medium: 10 min, Hard: 15 min, Expert: 20 min, Master: 25 min, Extreme: 30 min).

        Live Multiplayer Sudoku Tips

        Multiplayer Sudoku is faster and more competitive than solo play. Focus on easy sections first—rows, columns, or boxes with the most numbers filled.

        Avoid rushing. A small mistake can cost more time than checking your move. Stay accurate.

        Work in a structured way. Solve one section at a time to build speed and confidence.

        Winning Strategies for Competitive Play

        • Start with obvious placements—cells with only one possible number. These give quick progress.
        • Use elimination to rule out numbers quickly. The faster you narrow options, the faster you solve.
        • Skip tough areas and return later. They often become easier as the grid fills.
        • Practice regularly to improve speed and pattern recognition.

        Common Mistakes to Avoid

        • Don’t guess—always use logic.
        • Don’t ignore easy numbers while focusing on harder parts.
        • Stay focused. Distractions lead to mistakes.
        • Keep it simple. Basic logic solves most puzzles.

        Apply these tips to improve your speed and accuracy. Practice often and challenge other players to get better with every game.
