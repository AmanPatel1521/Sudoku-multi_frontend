How to Use the Solver

  1. Enter the known numbers from your puzzle
  2. Leave empty cells blank
  3. Click “Solve”
  4. Get the complete solution instantly

When Should You Use a Solver?

A Sudoku solver is helpful when:

  • You are completely stuck
  • You want to verify your solution
  • You are learning advanced strategies

Learn While Solving

Instead of just copying answers, try to:

  • Compare your approach with the solution
  • Understand where you made mistakes
  • Learn new solving patterns

Benefits of Using a Solver

  • Saves time on difficult puzzles
  • Helps you learn faster
  • Improves accuracy
  • Builds confidence

Important Tip

Use the solver as a learning tool, not a shortcut. Try solving the puzzle yourself first, then use the solver to check your work.
