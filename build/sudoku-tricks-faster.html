Top 15 Sudoku Tricks to Solve Faster

Proven techniques to slash your completion times and improve accuracy.

If you want to solve Sudoku puzzles quickly, you need more than basic knowledge. These proven techniques will help you improve speed and accuracy.

1. Start with Easy Numbers

Always begin with rows or columns that are almost complete.

2. Use Elimination Method

Eliminate impossible numbers to find the correct one.

3. Scan Rows and Columns

Quick scanning helps identify missing numbers faster.

4. Focus on 3×3 Boxes

Sometimes boxes reveal answers quicker than rows.

5. Avoid Guessing

Guessing slows you down and leads to mistakes.

6. Use Pencil Marks

Write small possible numbers to track options.

7. Look for Singles

Cells with only one possible number are easy wins.

8. Use Cross-Hatching

Check intersections of rows and columns.

9. Practice Pattern Recognition

Over time, you will recognize common patterns.

10. Stay Organized

Keep your solving method consistent.

11. Work Systematically

Don’t jump randomly across the grid.

12. Learn Advanced Techniques

Master strategies like X-Wing and Swordfish.

13. Stay Calm

Rushing leads to errors.

14. Play Daily

Consistency improves speed.

15. Try Multiplayer Sudoku

Competing with others improves performance under pressure.

Conclusion

Using these tricks consistently will significantly improve your Sudoku-solving speed.
